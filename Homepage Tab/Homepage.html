The Science of Excellence

At Pasig City Science High School, education is guided by a vision of becoming a world-class institution that is globally and locally competitive, especially in the fields of Science and Technology. The school is committed to developing well-rounded, science-oriented students who are nurtured to become gender-sensitive individuals and future leaders with a vision for excellence.


Through scholarship opportunities for deserving gifted students, a high-quality curriculum enriched with Science and Technology, and learning experiences that develop critical and creative thinking, PCSHS continues to prepare learners to meet the ever-changing needs of the times.
