History of PCSHS

History of PCSHS (Pasig City Science High School)

Pasig City Science High School was established through the vision of former City Mayor Soledad C. Eusebio, who, upon assuming office in 2001, sought to further strengthen the city's commitment to quality education. She envisioned a specialized science high school that would provide accessible and high-standard learning opportunities for academically gifted Pasigueños.

With the support of Hon. Mayor Vicente P. Eusebio and the members of the City Council, the school was formally founded on October 7, 2004, through City Ordinance No. 28, Series of 2004. It officially opened in School Year 2005-2006, temporarily holding classes on the upper floors of the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig while preparations for its permanent campus were underway. During its first year, students were admitted through a competitive screening process and granted full scholarship support from the city government.

Mr. Noel A. Salalima served as the school's first Principal, guiding the institution during its formative years. In 2006, the school transferred to its permanent six-storey building at Rainforest Park, equipped with modern classrooms and specialized laboratories to support advanced instruction in Science and Mathematics.

Since its establishment, Pasig City Science High School has remained steadfast in its mission to deliver specialized science education and to develop future leaders and innovators for Pasig City and the nation.
