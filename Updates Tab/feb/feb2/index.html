FEBRUARY

MR. FABABAER's APPOINTMENT AS ASDS, SDO MANILA

Previous Pasig City Science High School principal Mr. Charlie O. Fababaer, CESE who has recently served SDO Mandaluyong is starting a new chapter as Assistant Schools Division Superintendent (ASDS) at SDO Manila. Pasig City Science High School proudly congratulates him on this new chapter of leadership and service to education.
