31st DSPC GROUP EVENTS

The school congratulates the Pascian Campus Journalists who excellently represented Pasig City Science High School during the 31st DSPC Group Events last February 6, 2026 at Pasig Elementary School. They have garnered the following awards: Champion for Collaborative Desktop Publishing in English and 2nd Place for Radio Broadcasting in Filipino. The school extends its gratitude for the support of the administration and staff.
