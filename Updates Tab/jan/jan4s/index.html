JANUARY

DIVISION FESTIVAL OF TALENTS

The 2026 Division Festival of Talents conducted last January 14-16 has successfully concluded, and the school emerged as the Overall First Runner-up for the Secondary Level. Notable achievements also include garnering 1st place for STEMazing and Cinemunti, 2nd place for Speak Up! And Likhawit, 3rd Place for Bidyokasiya and Read-a-thon: Advocacy Pitch, and 5th Place for HistoPop. The students involved in the recently concluded competition have brought great pride and honor to the school.
