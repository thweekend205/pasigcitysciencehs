JANUARY

9 CCTVs INSTALLATION

Last January 7, 2026, the School Parent-Teacher Association (SPTA), headed by Mrs. Jennifer-Lopez Bagaoisan installed 9 additional CCTV cameras in the school. According to Pasig City Science High School, “this initiative significantly strengthens the safety, security, and protection of our learners, personnel, and other stakeholders”. The school expresses their sincere thanks to the organization and their support.
