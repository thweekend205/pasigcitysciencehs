UP ALCHEMES RESEARCH FAIR

Student researchers of Pasig City Science High School have once again brought pride to the Pascian Community through the recently conducted UP ALCHEMES Research Fair 2026 at the University of the Philippines Diliman, last January 15-17, 2026. The team was composed of Marie Claire Beloy, Sophia Nicole Santiago, and Justine Mikyla Clet, where they presented their study on “Anticancer Activity and Induction of Ti Plant (Cordyline fruticosa) Leaf Methanolic Crude Extract Against Cervical Cancer Cells (HeLa)”.
