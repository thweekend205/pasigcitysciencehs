BRIGADA ESKWELA '25: SAMA-SAMA PARA SA BAYANG BUMABASA

Before the start of A.Y. 2025–2026, Pasig City Science High School launched Brigada Eskwela 2025: Sama-sama para sa Bayang Bumabasa, a week-long bayanihan from June 9 to June 13,2026, where the PaScian community—students, parents, teachers, staff, and volunteers—gathered to clean, repair, and prepare the school for the new academic year. The event featured a kick-off parade, gardening activities, donation drives, workshops, and a mini pop-up shop, alongside scheduled cleaning of classrooms, hallways, stairways, comfort rooms, and school grounds. Each day was organized by grade level and committees, ensuring that all areas of the campus were covered. Through this collective effort, the event did more than just prepare a cleaner, safer environment; it forged lasting bonds and established secure spaces for every PaScian.
