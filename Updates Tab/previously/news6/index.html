STUDIO FRESHIE: FRESHIECON 2025

Yet another year to build connections through shared interest, Studio Freshie: FreshieCon 2025 was held on July 4, 2025, introducing Grade 7 and Grade 11 "freshies" to the vibrant heart of the PaScian community. Inspired by the artistry of Studio Ghibli, the event celebrated authentic human creativity as a stand against generative AI, featuring an inspiring talk by alumna Ms. Airene De Luna and high-energy hosting by Edwil Jay Pederal and Ingrid Faye Sarmiento. Both academic and non-academic clubs actively endorsed themselves through creative presentations, showcasing their unique missions to spark the journey of every freshie. Supported by the SSLG, faculty, and alumni, this collective effort successfully set the stage for the new students to excel, serve, and lead.
