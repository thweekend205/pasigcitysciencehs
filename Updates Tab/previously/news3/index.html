2NEI: 21st FOUNDATION DAY

The 21st Foundation Day at Pasig City Science High School on November 7, brought together students, faculty, and alumni for a meaningful celebration of 21 years of Pascian pride and history. The event kicked off with the opening of various booths at 9:00 AM, followed by highly anticipated sports matches, including a Basketball Exhibition Game in the gym and a Volleyball Exhibition Game on the grounds. The afternoon featured the "Sugod Classroom!: Magbayanihan Tayo!" noontime show and lively jamming sessions led by various student performers.
