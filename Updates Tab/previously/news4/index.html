TEACHERS' MONTH 2025

In honor of the mentors who serve as pillars of life, discipline, and academics, Teachers’ Month 2025 was celebrated on October 3, 2025, at the PCSHS Gymnasium. The program transformed our dear teachers into the day’s central stars, showcasing them as talented, skilled, and competitive participants in a series of festive activities. The day began with a Mini-teacher Orientation at 7:00 AM in the classrooms, followed by a high-energy Zumba session and Team Games at the gymnasium. After a lunch sponsored by the PCSHS SPTA, the afternoon featured a "Teachers’ Walk" from the library to the gym and concluded with a vibrant Talent Show. From the heart of every PaScian, to the mentors who inspire us, we honor the dedication that turns our potential into excellence.
