SPORTS FOR VALUES CLINIC

On November 29, 2025, the Pasig City Science High School EsP Department, led by Dr. Leilani C. Lucero, held the “Sports for Values Clinic” to build discipline among young athletes. In partnership with Elevate and CCF Sandoval, the event featured veteran TNT Tropang Giga players Kim Cyril Aurin, RR Pogoy, and Poy Erram, who shared their personal journeys and helped train the students. Approved by Ms. Liza C. Caigoy, the program successfully integrated sports with core values to help strengthen the character of PaScian learners.
