SCIENCE MONTH

Pasig City Science High School officially kicked off Science Month 2025 this September, launching a vibrant, month-long celebration dedicated to discovery, innovation, and environmental stewardship. The event features several activities, including the Pascian Battle of the Brains and hands-on projects at the DIY Lab & Exhibit. Students can also hear from alumni during the Balik Scientist Talk or participate in the "Your Sci-Face Looks Familiar" costume event. The month concludes with a tree-planting activity for Green Action Day. These programs provide students with different ways to practice their skills and explore their interest in science.
