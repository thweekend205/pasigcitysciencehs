SPORTS FESTIVAL

Pasig City Science High School held its Sports Festival at the PCSHS Grounds to promote physical fitness and balance academics with recreation. From December 9 to 17, students from Grade 7 to Grade 12 competed in boys' and girls' categories, starting with elimination rounds and culminating in the final matches. The event aimed to showcase student talent while fostering teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. By bringing the whole school community together, the festival helped build school spirit and pride beyond the classroom.
