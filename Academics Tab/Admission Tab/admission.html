Applicants and students are advised to review the school’s policies regarding academic standing and retention. Understanding these policies will help students and parents stay informed about the academic expectations required to remain enrolled in the school.

Student Retention Policy

As per Sangguniang Panlunsod, Ordinance No. 28, Series of 2004, Pasig City Science High School is mandated to provide a more intensive and advanced secondary education program with emphasis on Science, Mathematics and Communication Arts and in order to safeguard the quality of education and of the graduates of this school, a Student Retention Policy is implemented for strict compliance. Hence, a minimum final grade requirement is observed.

Minimum Grade Requirement

A General Weighted Average of 85% with no final grade below 80% in any subject, is the minimum grade requirement for students to be retained for the succeeding school year.

Regular Student
  • A student who has satisfactorily met the minimum grade as stated above is considered a student of Regular Status and is automatically qualified for promotion to the next grade level.
Student on Probation
  • Any student who is not on probation and has no final grade below 80% but whose general weighted average is below 85% (rounded-off) will be enrolled on probation.
  • Any student who is not on probation and whose general weighted average is at least 85% but with a grade below 80% in just only one subject will be enrolled on probation.
  • A student who will be readmitted on probation in the next level for JHS and next semester for SHS will sign a Contract of Agreement together with their parents for a grade compliance requirement during card distribution.
  • A student on probation must meet the grade requirement of a student on a Regular Status in all subject areas to regain his/her status as a regular student. Otherwise, he/she loses the qualification for readmission.
  • A student on probation must attend the scheduled quarterly individual/group coaching sessions.
Student Not Qualified for Readmission
  • Any student who has received a final grade below 80% in at least two (2) subjects.
  • Any student who has received a final failing grade in any subject.
  • Any student who is enrolled on a probationary status and who is not able to regain his/her qualification for a regular student at the end of the school year.
*Note: Conditions mentioned above are applied per semester for SHS students.

Requirements

Grade 7

  1. Duly accomplished Application Form Grade 7: https://bit.ly/ApplicationFormGr7
  2. Original and photocopy of Grade 5 and Grade 6 Report Cards showing compliance with the following grade requirements:
    • Grade 5 - a final grade of at least 85% in English, Mathematics, and Science, and 83% in all other learning areas; and
    • Grade 6 - a grade of at least 85% in English, Mathematics, and Science for the first and second quarters, and 83% in all other learning areas.
    Note: For schools using letter or descriptive grading systems, a certification of numerical equivalents must be provided.
  3. Original and photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  4. Two (2) recent 2x2 ID photos with a white background name tag: (FAMILY NAME, FIRST NAME, MIDDLE INITIAL)
    5. ALL REQUIREMENTS MUST BE INSERTED IN A LONG BROWN ENVELOPE LABELED AS SAMPLE BELOW:

    DELA CRUZ, JUAN SANTOS
    MALE / 0912-345-6789
    PASIG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
  5. For the Parent/Guardian:
    • Proof of Residency
    Note: Proof of residency is no longer limited to Voter’s Certification. Applicants may now submit a photocopy of ANY ONE of the following:
    1. Valid ID with residential address
    2. Latest Voter’s Certification
    3. Latest Utility Billing Statement (under parent/guardian’s name)
    4. Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
